Rank Systems

What are Rank Systems?

Rank Systems are a way to organize the different belts/ranks in your school. If you teach a specific style of martial arts like BJJ, Taekwondo, Judo, etc, you can set that Rank System Up here, then add your ranks to it. We give you the ability to add an unlimited amount of Rank Systems with an unlimited amount of ranks in each.

If you have a seperate rank order for your pre-schoolers than your older students, you could even create seperate rank systems for each.

If you only teach one martial art, and you have one rank order for all of your students, we recommend having only one Rank System.
