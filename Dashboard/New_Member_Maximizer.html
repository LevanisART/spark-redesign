New Member Maximizer

What is the New Member Maximizer?

We all know that the first 90 to 180 days of a new member's journey are the most crucial. It is when the most drop-outs tend to happen. By keeping a close eye on your new members, you'll keep more members for a longer time, you'll make more of an impact, and your business will grow.

New Member Phases

New Member Phases are the steps a member will go through to stay training long term. You can create your own phases to make New Member Maximizer unique to your business. Click here to customize the New Member Phases.
