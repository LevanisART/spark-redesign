What is the Spark Kiosk App?

Have your Students take attendance on a tablet using our iOS or Android based app called "Spark Kiosk." Search for it in the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store.

The app allows your Students to see latest announcements, check into their class, see their progress towards their next rank... all quickly and easily. You can put your tablet in "Guided Access Mode" so you can prevent them from viewing other apps.
