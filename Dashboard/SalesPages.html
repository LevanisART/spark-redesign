Checkout Pages grow your business

 

What are Checkout Pages?

Checkout Pages are used to generate profits in your business. Like an online Order Form, sales page or simple shopping cart. Using Checkout Pages you can easily sell an item, a package of items, a service or many services or even recurring subscriptions/memberships. Collecting payment is as simple as sending out an email or text message, or even pasting a button on your website or social media.
